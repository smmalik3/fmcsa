Available FMCSA Systems

L&I

System used to enter and display licensing and insurance information regarding authorized for-hire motor carriers, freight forwarders, and property brokers

An information system that captures data from field offices and the source for FMCSA inspection, crash, compliance review, safety audit, and registration data

NCCDB

System for recording and reporting on household good, safety violation, hazardous material, cargo tank and passenger complaints

System used to query up-to-the-minute information on orders and verification of carrier information
